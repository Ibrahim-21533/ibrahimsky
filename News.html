Recent Milestones & Announcements

🔧 SKY Core v1.0 Released

July 26, 2025

We’ve released SKY Core v1.0! The release includes full voice command support in English & Kiswahili, Bluetooth control for robots, and local storage memory for reminders.

📲 Mobile App Interface Launched

July 15, 2025

We’ve finalized the MIT App Inventor interface with joystick, speech input, sliders, and toggle controls! SKY now works seamlessly on Android devices.

🤖 Robot Transformation Module Added

July 7, 2025

New robot transformation logic and servo mapping have been added. SKY can now transform your robot from car to humanoid using voice or app control!

🧠 SKY Now Has Memory System

June 30, 2025

SKY now remembers your name, birthday, ID info, reminders, and workout stats. You can ask, “What’s my ID number?” or “How many push-ups can I do?” and get instant responses.

🌍 Offline Mode & Kiswahili Language Support

June 21, 2025

Voice commands are now supported offline and in Kiswahili. Try saying: “Nikumbushe kufanya mazoezi saa kumi jioni”.
