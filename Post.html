Sky Project Update: July 2025

Posted on July 20, 2025 by Ibrahim Nyandwaro

The SKY AI Assistant is reaching new levels. After finalizing modules like voice commands, file sharing, reminders, and memory tracking, the next phase is full multi-platform integration.

Our focus is on building a smooth user experience across Android and PC. Bluetooth support, offline mode, and voice-activated controls are now stable. I’ll also be releasing the full `.aia` MIT App Inventor file for those interested in developing their own versions of SKY.

This project is more than software—it's the first step toward creating intelligent systems that understand and respond to the African context in language, design, and function.

Stay tuned for more technical breakdowns and behind-the-scenes updates.
